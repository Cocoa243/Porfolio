My journey with Cheetah Code

The first time I coded was last year (2020) when I joined the very first Cheetah Code bootcamp. One of my cousins who was already following the classes talked to me about it and convinced me to take part since I was on furlough and I had time to learn new skills. I was skeptical to follow the classes at first but then after a few lessons, I was starting to enjoy coding and learning new things. I wasn’t always easy to understand the logic behind certain operations or to directly understand everything during the lesson but we did learn a lot (basics of HTML and CSS) in a short period of time. We also strarted with JavaScript but it was very hard for me to understand therefore I am joining the Bootcamp again this year (2021).
